# Project Context

## Project Overview
LegaBot landing page - a modern, dark-themed marketing website for an AI-operated autonomous football universe. Target audience: investors, fans, and potential partners.

| Date | Decision | By | Rationale |
|------|----------|-----|-----------|

## Constraints
- Color Palette: Graphite Black #232323 (primary bg), Digital Violet #635cff (accent), Silver Gray #a3a3a3 (secondary text), Soft Gray #ebebed (light text), White #ffffff, Black #000000
- Typography: Poppins (Light, Regular, Medium, SemiBold, Bold, Black weights)
- Logo: White version on dark backgrounds, Violet version for accents
- Layout: Dark, modern, bold typography with violet highlights for emphasis words
- Animations: Smooth scroll, subtle fade-ins, modern glassmorphism cards
